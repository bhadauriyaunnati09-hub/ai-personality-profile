Day 24 – Startup Business Strategy Report

Startup Summary

Startup Name: AI Career Mentor

AI Career Mentor is an AI-powered platform designed to help students and fresh graduates navigate their career journey. The platform provides personalized career guidance, resume reviews, interview preparation, skill-gap analysis, and learning roadmaps.

Key Highlights

- AI-driven career recommendations
- Resume and LinkedIn profile analysis
- Personalized learning roadmaps
- Mock interview preparation
- Skill-gap identification
- Career progress tracking
- Affordable subscription model
- Focused on students and early professionals

---

Executive Summary

AI Career Mentor addresses the growing challenge faced by students and graduates who lack personalized career guidance. Traditional career counseling is often expensive, inaccessible, or generic.

The startup leverages AI to deliver scalable and affordable career coaching, helping users make informed career decisions and improve employability.

Business Goal: Become the preferred AI-powered career guidance platform for students and job seekers.
